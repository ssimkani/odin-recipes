Sausage Balls

Description:

These amazing sausage balls are sure to elevate any Thanksgiving meal!
It's only three ingredients and they're a real crowd pleaser, made with a savory sausage, store-bought biscuit, and some sharp cheddar cheese.
The taste is unbelievable and it's cheesy flavor can't be beaten!

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound ground pork sausage, at room temperature
  • 2 cups biscuit baking mix
  • 1 pound sharp Cheddar cheese, shredded

Steps:

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F
  2. Combine sausage and biscuit mix in a large bowl; mix with hands until they are well combined
  3. Add cheddar cheese and mix until fully incorporated
  4. Roll the mixture into 30 walnut-sized balls; transfer them to a baking sheet
  5. Bake in the preheated oven until it's golden brown and sausage is cooked through, 20 to 25 minutes

Juicy Thanksgiving Turkey

Description:

An amazingly tender and juicy meat thats a stable in any Thanksgiving meal.
Orange wedges are used to add flavor while some champagne broth keeps the turkey moist and tender.

Ingredients:

  • tablespoons dried parsley
  • tablespoons ground dried rosemary
  • tablespoons rubbed dried sage
  • tablespoons dried thyme leaves
  • tablespoon lemon-pepper seasoning
  • tablespoon salt
  • (15 pound) whole turkey, neck and giblets removed
  • medium orange, cut into 8 wedges
  • medium onion, chopped into large pieces
  • medium carrot, cut into 1/2-inch slices
  • stalks celery, cut into 1/2-inch slices
  • 1 bottle of champagne
  • (14.5 ounce) can chicken broth

Steps:

  1. Gather all ingredients and preheat oven to 350 degrees F. Wrap roaster with sheets of aluminum foil that are long enough to wrap around the turkey.
  2. Stir the parsley, rosemary, sage, thyme, lemon-pepper seasoning, and salt in a small bowl.
  3. Rub herb mixture into turkey cavity, then stuff with orange wedges, onion, carrot, and celery. Tie turkey legs together with kitchen string. Tuck the wings under the body.
  4. Place turkey on the foil in the roasting pan, then pour champagne and chicken broth over the turkey and make sure to get some in the cavity.
  5. Bring the aluminum foil up and over the turkey and seal it.
  6. Roast turkey in preheated oven for 2.5 to 3 hours until juices run clear. Uncover the turkey and continue baking for another 30 to 60 minutes until skin turns golden brown.
  7. Remove the turkey from the oven, cover it with two sheets of aluminum and let it rest in a warm area for 15 minutes before slicing.
  8. Serve and Enjoy!
